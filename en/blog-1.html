1. What is a sales funnel and why use it?

A sales funnel is a sequence of web pages designed to guide a visitor from discovering your offer to purchasing, and even upselling. It's an essential tool for selling online 24/7 without human intervention.

Advantages:

  • Automation of sales and email marketing
  • Increased conversion rates
  • Collection and segmentation of qualified leads
  • Quick monetization of an offer even without a website

2. Architecture of a high-performing Systeme.io funnel

A well-designed funnel should guide the user smoothly with one objective per page. Here are the essential elements:

  • Capture page: offer a lead magnet (ebook, checklist, etc.)
  • Sales page: clear structure, storytelling, call-to-action
  • Order page: simple form, guarantees, social proof
  • Upsell/downsell page: complementary offer or good deal
  • Thank you page: instructions + sharing proposal or action

3. Step-by-step tutorial to create a Systeme.io funnel

  1. Log in to your Systeme.io account
  2. Go to the Sales Funnels tab and click "Create"
  3. Choose the goal: Build your audience or Sell a product
  4. Select a template or start from scratch
  5. Add your pages and customize each step (texts, visuals, buttons)
  6. Define automations (emails triggered based on actions)
  7. Connect your payment gateway (Stripe, PayPal)

In less than 30 minutes, you can have an active and functional funnel ready to convert your visitors.

4. Conversion optimization: 7 advanced techniques

  • Countdown timers: urgency effect to motivate action
  • A/B testing: compare two versions of your page
  • Limited offers: number of spots or access time
  • Social proof: testimonials, security badges, press logos
  • Color psychology: blue = trust, red = action
  • Mini demonstration video: boosts engagement
  • Facebook/Google pixel tracking: intelligent retargeting
